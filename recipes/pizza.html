Pizza

Description

This recipe wll show you the easiest way to create the most delicious pizz you will ever taste!

This meal will be ready within minutes, read on to see how to cook this meal your whole fmaily will love

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 package of dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat your oven to 450 degrees
  2. Place warm water in a bowl, pour in the yeast and let sit for 10 minutes
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
  5. turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface
  6. Transfer ontoa piza pan
  7. Spread crust with toppings of your choice, along with chees
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown
